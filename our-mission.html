Our Mission: Stay Longer Off-line

Our mission is to help you spend more time away from your phone and your computer, in your physical environment. With the people you love, and the strangers you meet. To be present and fully aware of the fleeing moments of the only life you have. To smell the coffee, to taste of your food, the see the look in your dogâ€™s eyes and the colors of the clouds, to feel cold breeze, the sun, and the rain. To create time and attention to read again, to play with your cat, and talk to people around you.

Online life is not life

Online technologies accessible through our phones provide unlimited entertainment. From funny clips, to amazing games, to beautiful documentaries. Experiences beyond your wildest dreams from the comfort of your couch .

But even the most immersive online game is one-dimensional. Real life still happens in the physical world that engages all your senses, forms your personality, your dreams and your memories. It is not always easier, nor as addictive, and certainly not always comfortable to get off the couch and walk into the park, but it is ultimately more engaging, rewarding, and certainly healthier.

Ask yourself, or ask your friend to list the top 5 experiences from a week, a month, a year or a decade ago. Will anyone list the clips they watched on Instagram or tiktok? In hindsight, most would give up those thousands of forgotten lonely hours for more of our real experiences, sports games where you were pushed to your limit, holidays where you made new friends, lunch with family, or even jointly watching television with no phone connection.

Towards a healthier online diet

While online technologies have delivered many benefits, they have become too good in grabbing and holding our attention for too long. For most of us, our discipline is no match to the algorithms made to keep us engaged. The phone in our pocket that is not only screaming for attention, we often really need it. To check our agenda, the weather or the list or reminders. And every time we check, we easily get lost again in the metaverse.

AWE panels place some of the information you need in your physical environment, away from your phone and its endless distractions.

What? Screens to replace screens...?

Indeed. That sounds paradoxical. But AWE screens are made to integrate with your environment. No private screens that take everyone to their own online world but screens for others to see. To set-up and forget. Quietly part of your environment. Paper-like greyscale e-ink screens with up to 6 months of battery time. The panels are intentionally non-interactive. No swiping up or down, no likes, no recommendations, no notifications to worry about, no click bait news articles, no voices and sounds trying to grab your attention. Nothing to grab your attention and hijack your brain.

You choose in advance what you really need to see, and these limitations will translate into free time for you and attention that can be directed to your surroundings.

Aiding your memory

Memory is spatial and with AWE you can utilize this. Not only can you place the information there were you need it. Todays weather next to your dressing closet, your daily calendar when you wake up, and your stocks in your study. Even if you place all screens on one wall, the relative location, and the background picture will help you remember the information better.

Aiding your sanity

Besides a better memory, the ability for AWE to remove reasons to check your time on your phone, and reduce the time spend on the internet should help you in many other ways. From increased ability to focus, to eyes that are less tired, stress that is reduced, to healthier relationships, and sleep that improves. That is the mission.
